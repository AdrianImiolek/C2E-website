about us

We are a team of passionate escape room fanatics dedicated to bringing the thrill of real-life puzzle-solving into the digital world. We love the rush of cracking codes, uncovering hidden clues, and piecing together mysteries, and we channel that passion into crafting immersive point-and-click games. Our debut title, Trace: Trinity’s Code, is currently in development. We don’t have a release date just yet, but we’re working hard to bring it to you as soon as possible. If you enjoy clever puzzles, immersive worlds, and stories that keep you guessing – stay tuned. There’s a lot to come.
